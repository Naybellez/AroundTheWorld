Let's travel to... Sölden, Austria!

What is there to do?

  • Snowboarding, duh!
  • I guess you could ski too...
  • There is also ice skating, and sledding, and even paraskiing!
  • Any sport with snow or ice, you'll find it here
  • You don't need ski's to visit the glacier, take the lift up and marvel at the views
  • Après ski! After a long day on the mountains, what better way to relax than with a cold beer, a mulled wine or maybe even a cheeky shot of jägermeister (you deserve it)
  • To recharge, I would go to one of the many self-service restaurants on the mountains where you'll find plenty of reasonably priced food (you just have to find a place to sit first- good luck)
  • There are also plenty of restaurants to choose from, I recommend Gusto for pizza, it is to die for
Seriously you should try snowsports, it's going to get harder and harder in the future to go on holiday since the world is getting hotter and hotter and all the snow is going to melt and then we're basically screwed, too dark? Enjoy the pretty pictures anyway.
